Who We Are

Pencil Line (Private) Limited is an International Freight Forwarding and Customs Clearing company proudly based in Harare, Zimbabwe. We specialise in the seamless movement of cargo across borders and work closely with clients to ensure compliance, speed and transparency throughout the logistics process.

We handle Customs Clearance, Freight Forwarding and Port Operations with a focus on professionalism, reliability and timely delivery. Our team is driven by a passion for efficient logistics solutions, offering tailored services that meet both individual and corporate needs.

At Pencil Line Freight, we believe in Quality, Innovation and Flexibility. By staying up-to-date with customs regulations, industry trends and new technologies, we ensure that our clients experience smooth and stress-free cargo processing from origin to destination.
